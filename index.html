About

Design has and continues to give me the ability to pursue both a creative and open-minded approach to all facets of my life, from the clothing I buy to the nutrition tracker I use, to choosing Doordash or Postmates. Design has empowered me to live critically and consciously, to collaborate effectively, to empathize deeply; it has irrevocably changed my view of the world; it has gifted me with the importance of realizing that progress is not linear but is always on an upward trajectory.

As a biracial woman born and raised in Brooklyn, I grew up witnessing how diverse societies allow us to be productive innovators. As designers and researchers, our designs cannot produce an effective user experience without empathy for all users.

I seek to further foster a user base that rises above biases, removes complacency, and ethnic representation through the user experience.

I love indulging my analytical and problem-solving side by finding code based solutions while also being able to enact my creative design muscles. If you’re as passionate about UX design, UX trends, and want to learn more about me and my experiences, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
